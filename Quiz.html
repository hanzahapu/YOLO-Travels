Kinniya Bridge is the longest bridge in Sri Lanka with a length of 396 metres (​1,299 ft), located in Trincomalee.

Pidurutalagala stands at 8,281 feet making it the tallest mountain in the country, located near Nuwara Eliya city.

The Nil Manel (Nymphaea stellata) Blue Water Lily was chosen as the national flower of Sri Lanka in February 1986.

The Sri Lankan junglefowl (Gallus lafayettii), also known as the Ceylon junglefowl is the national bird of Srilanka.

The Sacred Tooth Relic (Dālada) of the Buddha is the most venerated object of worship for Buddhists. Its housed in the Temple of the Tooth Relic (Dālada Māligāwa) in Kandy.

The tallest skyscraper Lotus Tower also known as “Nelum Kuluna” is 350 meters tall, located in the heart of Colombo city. This tower is currently the tallest self-supported structure in South Asia.
